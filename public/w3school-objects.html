Using an Object Literal

Example of Objects

In real life, objects are things like houses, cars, people, animals, or any other subjects.

Here is a car object example:

Properties

  • car.name: Tesla
  • car.model: Model Y
  • car.weight: 2,003kg
  • car.color: Black

Methods

  • car.start(): Starts the car
  • car.drive(): Drives the car
  • car.brake(): Stops the car
  • car.stop(): Turns off the car
