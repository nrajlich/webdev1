What is Rebus Design?

A rebus is a puzzle device that combines the use of illustrated pictures with individual letters to depict words or phrases. In design, a rebus plays with visual elements to convey a message or a name, often requiring the viewer to solve a puzzle to understand the intended message. This technique combines both visual and textual elements to engage the audience in an interactive way, pushing them to piece together the meaning behind the visual clues presented..
